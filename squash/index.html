Announcements

Jan 8, 2025:
 Happy new year !
 Welcome to 2025. With my comprehensive exam approaching and recovery of the knee, I will only commit to Friday squashes each week until March.
In the meantime, for those looking for a friendly competitive outlet and willing to commit a moderate amount of time:

  • Jan 20 to Mar 2 — Box League:
     At the beginning of each week you are placed into a 'box' and arrange times to play each other amongst yourselves before the end of the week. The usual Friday times are discouraged. At the end of the week, the player(s) are either promoted or demoted to above/below boxes before the next week starts and the cycle repeats. Matches are 1v1 rules: best-of-three games with each game being a first-to-eleven win by two format. Players can expect to be matched with one to two other players in their box (depending on how many people sign up).

  • March 3 to March 30 — Elimination Bracket:
     A 1v1 standard double elimination bracket where players arrange times to play amongst themselves within a 1-week timeframe.
    - Billy
